General features

  • Material Properties
    • Material type: DKP sheet, galvanized sheet, painted galvanized sheet
    • Material width (mm): 46mm
    • Material thickness (mm): 0.20 (min) - 0.40 (max)
    • Roll weight: 2 tons capacity
    • Inner diameter of roll (mm): 500
    • Outer diameter of roll (mm): 1500
  • Line Properties
    • Line speed (m / min): 70mt / min (maximum)
  • Product features
    • Length tolerance: +/- 1 mm
  • Power, Voltage, Current Properties
    • 3 phase voltage (V): 380
    • Total power on line (kW): 15
  • Pneumatic
    • Rated working pressure (bar): 8
  • Paint
    • Operations: anticorrosion -> primer -> steel paste -> topcoat (color of fixed parts: Ral 5012 (blue))
  • Warranty
    • 1 (one) year after delivery.

Line Elements

  • Uncoiler
    • Band width (mm): 100
    • Roll carrier capacity (kg): 500
    • Outer diameter of the roll (mm): 1200
    • Roll inner diameter (mm): 500-600 adjustable.
  • Eccentric Press
    • An air clutch press with a capacity of 40 tons will be used.
    • The stroke capacity is 200 - 250.
    • The press will be controlled by a 3 kW drive.
    • The punching die is 2379 quality steel and the wire will be eroded and the piercing pins will be made of HSS steel.
  • Material Channel
    • There will be 1 material channel at the uncoiler output.
    • There will be 1 material channel at the eccentric press outlet.
  • Roll form Machine
    • 6 station unit.
    • Stations will be produced from 1040 quality material.
    • Black phosphate coating will be made.
    • Station shafts will be produced from 4140 reclamation steel. The materials shall be treated first and then subjected to induction process and shall be subjected to surface hardness grinding.
    • Form reels will be manufactured from 4340 quality material. The production method is first normalized and then subjected to CNC process, 60 HRC hardness is given, and then surface hard turning will be done.
    • There shall be units on the machine for the linearity and calibration of the product.
    • Drive gears shall be manufactured from 8620 cementation steel.
    • The movement to the stations is provided by moving with a chain from 3 kW geared motor and giving it to the gears.
  • Cutting Unit
    • It shall be manufactured to be able to cut between 0.50m - 3.3m.
    • Cutting dies shall be made of 2379 Swedish steel with high shear strength. The wire is processed in erosion and 62 HRC hardness is gained. Vacuum system is heat treated.
    • Cutting system will be provided with 80x100mm stroke pneumatic. It works with 8 bar air.
  • Packing Unit
    • The resulting profiles will be stacked properly and bonded to the roll form outlet.
  • Control System
    • The profile length of the PLC operator panel, the amount of profile to be produced and the production speed parameters will be entered and the production data will be stored in memory.

Safety

  • Mold part of the press unit will be closed with transparent thick PVC plastic cover window.
  • There shall be emergency stop buttons on the band opening machine, press, roll form machine and packaging unit.
  • The warning light will come on when the line starts.
  • Hazardous parts in motion will have enclosures.
  • An automatic stop will be activated during overload.
