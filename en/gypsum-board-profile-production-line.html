General features

  • Material Properties
    • Material type: DKP sheet, galvanized sheet, painted galvanized sheet
    • Material width (mm): 350
    • Material thickness (mm): 0.20 (min) - 1.50 (max)
    • Roll weight (kg): 5000
    • Inner diameter of the roll (mm): 500-600
    • Outer diameter of the roll (mm): 1200
  • Line Properties
    • Line speed (m / min): 100mt / min (maximum) (profile without holes)
    • Line speed (m / min): 60mt / min (maximum) (perforated profile)
  • Product Features
    • Length tolerance: +/- 1 mm
    • The molds for the following 9 forms will be delivered with the machine.
      • • Ceiling C Profile 60 mm
      • • Ceiling U Profile 28 mm
      • Note: Profile patterns will be specified separately.
  • Power, Voltage, Current Properties
    • 3 phase voltage (V): 380
    • Total power on line (kW): 35
  • Paint
    • Operations: anticorrosion -> primer -> steel putty -> topcoat (color of fixed parts: Ral 5012 (blue))
  • Warranty
    • 1 (one) year after delivery.
  • The most important feature of our machine is that the transition from mold to mold takes 5 minutes. We deliver all the reels on the machine.

Line Elements

  • Uncoiler
    • Band width (mm): 350
    • Roll carrier capacity (kg): 5000
    • Outer diameter of the roll (mm): 1200
    • Roll inner diameter (mm): 500-600 adjustable.
    • The drive will be controlled by the system.
    • There shall be mechanical brake.
  • Roll form Machine
    • The unit consists of 12 stations.
    • Stations will be produced from 1040 quality material.
    • Black phosphate coating will be made.
    • Station shafts will be produced from 4140 reclamation steel. The materials shall be treated first and then subjected to induction process and shall be subjected to surface hardness grinding.
    • Form reels will be manufactured from 4340 quality material. The production method is first normalized and then subjected to CNC process, 60 HRC hardness is given, and then surface hard turning will be done.
    • Drive gears shall be manufactured from 8620 cementation steel.
    • The movement to the stations is provided by taking the movement from the 18 kW geared motor with worm gearbox and giving it to the gears.
    • The gears shall be in the oil chamber.
  • Cutting Unit-Flying Scissors
    • It shall be manufactured to be able to cut between 0.50m - 12m.
    • Cutting dies shall be made of 2379 Swedish steel with high shear strength. The wire is processed in erosion and 62 HRC hardness is gained. Vacuum system is heat treated.
    • The cutting system is hydraulic with a capacity of 7,5 tons and will be controlled by servo motor drive.
  • Drilling Press
    • It shall be manufactured in such a way that it can work without stopping the production line.
    • The hole punching press is hydraulically driven and will have a drilling capacity of 7,5 tons.
    • Cutting dies shall be made of 2379 Swedish steel with high shear strength. The wire is processed in erosion and 62 HRC hardness is gained. Vacuum system is heat treated.
  • Packing Unit
    • The resulting profile will be pushed to the tilting table by conveyor belt.
    • The profiles will be stacked properly and bonded to the roll form outlet. Operation of the movement of the table given by the piston with the cutting of the profile with the right movement of the profile will fall.
  • Control System
    • The profile length of the PLC operator panel, the amount of profile to be produced and the production speed parameters will be entered and the production data will be stored in memory.

Safety

  • Roll form drive system will be covered with sheet metal.
  • There shall be emergency stop buttons on the band opening machine, press, roll form machine and packaging unit.
  • The warning light will come on when the line starts.
  • Hazardous parts in motion will have enclosures.
  • An automatic stop will be activated during overload.
